About Us

Brightly brings everyone in the education community together all in one place. We create
technology, content and platforms that connect teachers, students and parents to each
other and help all learners reach their full potential.

Our Values

We believe online learning has the power to improve education and enrich the lives of
everyone in the education community , anywhere and anytime. We put the needs of
teachers and students first in everything we build and do.

Our Mission

Our goal is to connect students with the people and resources they need to reach their full potential. Over
the last ten years as the classroom itself has become so much more than a physical space, we’ve
developed some amazing technology that teachers love and depend on. But we know we have so much
more to do and we’re always looking ahead to how we can improve the learning outcomes of entire
communities.

Brightly is a global educational platform that helps connect
students with teachers and resources for effective learning
needed for them to reach their full potential.
